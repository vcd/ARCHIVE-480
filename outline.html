Assignments

Reading and writing assignments utilizing required text, provided texts, and the PenFlip service:

  • Required Texts
    • Design is A Job
    • You're My Favorite Client by Mike Monteiro
  • Provided Texts
    • Excerpts from How They Got There by Khoi Vinh
    • Do Not Purchase. Excerpts will be provided

Exercises

Exercises are intended to practice and apply various topics outlined. These include:

  1. Your Company One Sheet
  2. Contract Writing
  3. Expenses & Revenue
  4. Pitching Work
  5. Creative Brief

Projects

Projects are opportunities to practice and apply outlined topics. These individual projects also aim to distinguish your portfolio.

Project I: Competition Entry

Students will submit an entry - for instructor review - design competition. Dates and details for The Pizzazz of Print design will be provided.

Project II: Individual Project

Students will find, contact, and attain new business projects. These will be presented in class as projects are completed.

  • Pitching and establishing (contract) new business projects
  • Presenting, and collaborating with clients on their projects
  • Time tracking, and project management
  • Accounting: payments, expenses, and invoices

Project III: Designers Choice

Same as Project I or Project II. Choose how you want to build your portfolio. Find a new work, or choose from:

  • Graphic Competitions
  • Comm Arts
  • AIGA
  • Contest Watchers
